## 👨‍🔬 About Me

I am a researcher and engineer specializing in **Computational and Data Science**, with a strong focus on advanced machine learning, state-space models, and distributed systems. 

* 🧠 **Currently building:** Baby Dragon Hatchling (BDH), a novel scale-free state-space sequence-learning architecture using spiking neural networks.
* 🛰️ **Recent research:** Engineered AI radar signal-strength prediction systems for PSLV/SSLV atmospheric-interference detection at **ISRO (Satish Dhawan Space Centre)**.
* ⚡ **Industry focus:** Validating production-grade LLM pipelines, agentic workflows (LangGraph), and RLHF evaluation rubrics as an AI Training Specialist.
* 🔭 **Interests:** Lunar SAR Polarimetry, Spiking Neural Networks, Big Data ETL, and Mechanistic Interpretability.
